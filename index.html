Dear Friends,

“I show up like a friend, plan like a pro, and care like a mom.”

Let's get one thing straight; I'm not here to plan your party like I'm producing a royal wedding in a castle with 47 spreadsheets and a balloon budget that requires a loan officer.

No.

I'm the mood director.
The vibe curator.
The “why does this backyard suddenly feel like an underwater Netflix special?” person.

Because I've always been the one adding one more silly detail. Another shell. Another prop. Another unnecessary-but-actually-necessary moment of magic.

And honestly? Kids don't need “luxury event planning.” It's a birthday party, not a Met Gala with juice boxes.

Save the thousands for the wedding. I'm serious. Don't spend college tuition on a cake that's going to get smashed into a toddler's eyebrow in 12 seconds.

So I created MerMommy Creations, where the magic is already done for you.
The concepts? Done.
The vibe? Locked.
The accessories, props, and little ocean-world chaos? Ready to rent like you're borrowing Atlantis for the afternoon.

You just show up and suddenly your backyard is giving “mermaid DJ set, emotional support seagulls, and questionable crab behavior.”

Because childhood should feel magical, not expensive.

So come on in. Let's make it cute, silly, a little ridiculous, and unforgettable.

Side effects may include smiling, squealing, and excessive shell-fies.

With love from the tide,
Kayte
